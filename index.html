Chain link challenge- tutor edition

Connect words in a chain - Guess the next word that connects to the previous one

How to play:

1. Enter a list of words for each player (at least 3 words each)

2. Click "Start Game" to begin

3. Each player gets their own set of words

4. Reveal letters one by one with "Next Letter" or show the whole word with "Reveal"

5. First player to guess all their words wins!
